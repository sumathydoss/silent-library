Whispers Between Pages

by Laila Nor | Contemporary Fiction | 2021

In the quiet corners of a modern eLibrary, an introverted archivist discovers a handwritten journal hidden inside an old poetry book. As she pieces together its forgotten story, she unravels truths about her own past and the people she's distanced herself from. A gentle, heartwarming novel about memory, solitude, and second chances.
