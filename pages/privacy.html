Privacy Policy

At Silent Library, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) of Singapore.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when registering for membership, subscribing to newsletters, or using our digital services. This may include your name, email, contact number, and borrowing history.

2. Use of Information

Your information is used to:

  • Manage your library membership and book transactions
  • Provide access to eLibrary and event registrations
  • Send you updates about library news, promotions, and events
  • Improve user experience and library services

3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties. Information may be shared with trusted partners only when necessary for service delivery (e.g., event registrations, eBook platforms), and in compliance with legal requirements.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. This includes secure servers, encryption, and limited access protocols.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. You may also opt out of our mailing lists.

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and track usage patterns. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. All changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
