The Quiet Path

by Nadia Chen | Fiction | 1996

In a fast-moving world full of noise and distraction, The Quiet Path follows the life of Eliya, a former city journalist who retreats to a forgotten coastal village in search of clarity. What begins as a temporary escape slowly transforms into a journey of inner healing, unexpected friendships, and a rediscovery of purpose through silence, nature, and books. With lyrical prose and reflective moments, this story is a gentle reminder that sometimes, the most meaningful journeys are taken not with our feet — but with our hearts.
